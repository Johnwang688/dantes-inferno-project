Layer 2: Gluttony

Ingredient: Soggy Bread Pudding Soaked in Excess Syrup

Significance: Gluttons indulged excessively in food and drink, prioritizing appetite over reason. Their contrapasso forces them to lie in freezing, filthy rain and mud while Cerberus gnaws at them. The soggy bread pudding ruined by too much syrup symbolizes how excess pleasure becomes its own punishment. The dripping, cold wetness of the pudding mirrors the rain and mud in layer 3. This ingredient shows how excess pleasure becomes its own punishment when taken beyond moderation.

"I am in the third circle, in the cloud of rain eternal, cursed, cold, and falling heavy, unchangin beat, unchanging quality. Thick hail and dirty water mixed with snow come down in torrents through the murky air, and the earth is stinking from this soaking rain."
— Canto VI, lines 7–12
