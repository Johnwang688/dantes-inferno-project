Layer 4: Violence

Ingredient: Spicy Chili Chocolate Ganache (Red)

Significance: Sinners here committed violence against others, self, or God/nature, including murderers, suicides, and blasphemers. Punishments include immersion in boiling blood, fiery rain, or burning sands. The bright red, intensely spicy chili-chocolate ganache burns the mouth when consumed, reflecting the blood and flames that the sinners are forced to endure. The vivid red color reinforces the imagery of blood and fire that dominate layer 7.

"...the river of blood that boils the souls of those who through their violence injured others."
— Canto XII, lines 47–48
